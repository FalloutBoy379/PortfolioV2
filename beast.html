Beast Electric Delivery Truck

Developed by Accelo Mobility, the Beast Truck embodies the company’s mission to drive sustainable transportation. Aimed at replacing traditional tempo vans, the truck offers a payload capacity of 2 tons with a range of up to 150 kilometers. Officially launched in May 2022, it attracted interest from companies like Delhivery.

Why Accelo Stood Out

Working at Accelo Mobility gave me the opportunity to collaborate with a dynamic and driven team that is deeply committed to fostering sustainable transportation solutions. As a company that champions the ‘Make in India’ initiative, Accelo strives to develop cutting-edge technology in-house, reflecting their dedication to innovation and self-reliance.

Accelo’s focus on electrification of the logistics market, particularly last-mile delivery, is aimed at reducing carbon footprints and lowering operational costs for businesses. During my time there, I witnessed the company’s ambitious expansion plans, including the release of around 100 electric vehicles by the end of the year.

"Accelo reduces operational costs by 30-35% because the vehicles are electric, and maintenance costs are just 1/5th of diesel vehicles."

Vrunda Nimje, Co-Founder, Accelo Mobility

"Priced between INR 6.5 lakh to INR 9 lakh, the vehicle will come in multiple variants to serve different use cases. The base model, focused on last-mile delivery, offers a 150 km range with a 15kW motor."

Arbaz Reza, CEO, Accelo Mobility

My Role & Contributions

  • Developed the wire harness and integrated steering stalk controls for seamless driving operations.
  • Designed PCB layouts managing both high voltage and low voltage DC components to avoid interference.
  • Collaborated with the Battery Management System (BMS) team to ensure smooth communication across subsystems.
  • Integrated ESP32 SIM800L for server connectivity, pushing real-time vehicle performance metrics to a database.
  • Developed GPS-based tracking for fleet management, enabling centralized control of the entire fleet.
  • After launch, upgraded to a Raspberry Pi-based system for in-vehicle tracking software with enhanced network connectivity.
  • Learned and applied crimping techniques with connectors designed for high-current applications.

Key Innovations

  • Equipped with extra-bright LED lighting, featuring a powerful lightbar as the main headlight.
  • ESP32 module enabled remote performance monitoring, fleet tracking, and visual analytics.
  • Seamlessly integrated vehicle data with Accelo Mobility's tool suite, providing customers with actionable insights.

Performance Overview

The Beast truck proved to be a highly efficient solution for last-mile delivery. With a payload capacity of 2 tons, it surpassed existing tempo vans while offering a greener alternative for logistics.

Its GPS tracking system enabled real-time fleet management, while data logging and analysis tools offered customers deep insights into performance and operational efficiency.
